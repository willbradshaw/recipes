Name: Banarasi aloo
Path: image/PXL_20260503_115610208.jpg
Book: Curry Easy Vegetarian
Rating: 4/5
Complexity: 2/5
Servings: 6
Calories/serving: 355

Ingredients:

100ml vegetable oil
1/2 tsp asaf
1 tsp cumin seeds
2 tsp mustard seeds
4 tsp ginger
3-4 chillies
3 tsp coriander
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp chilli powder
1350 potatoes
4 tsp amchur
3 tsp salt
3 tsp MSG
2 tsp garam masala
No added water anywhere

Process changes:

Chop potatoes into dice while raw without peeling, boil chunks for 30 minutes
Add asaf & seeds all together as a spice mix
Add salt and MSG with coriander, turmeric, chilli powder
