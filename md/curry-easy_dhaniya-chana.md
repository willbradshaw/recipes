<div class="title-block">
# Dhaniya chana
## Chickpeas in fresh coriander sauce
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 126

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 5

**Calories/serving:** 245

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums

**Other notes:** Simple, but has a long cook time.
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* **Sauce paste:**
    * 15 ml lime or lemon juice
    * 1 medium tomato
    * 50g fresh coriander leaves and tender stems
    * 1-2 fresh hot green chillies
    * 3 tsp fresh ginger
    * 1 teaspoon salt
    * 45 ml water
* 1 medium onion
* 3 × 16oz (~450g) tins of chickpeas
* 60 ml vegetable oil
* ½ tsp asafoetida
* 1 tsp cumin seeds
* **Spice mix:**
    * 1 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp MSG
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Combine the **sauce paste** ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth
2. Peel and finely chop the **onion**.
3. Drain the **chickpeas**.
4. Prepare the **spice mix**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a non-stick frying pan over a medium heat.
2. Add the **asafoetida** and, a few seconds later, the **cumin seeds**. Let them sizzle for a few seconds.
3. Add the **onion** and fry for 7-8 minutes, until slightly browned at the edges.
4. Add the **spice mix** and stir for 30 seconds.
5. Add the **chickpeas** and 200 ml water. Stir and bring to a simmer, then cover and simmer gently for 20 minutes.
6. Stir in the **sauce paste** and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook gently for another 20 minutes.
7. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
