<div class="title-block">
# Jeera aloo
## Potatoes with cumin and asafoetida
</div>

<div class="preamble">

**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 99

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 6

**Calories/serving:** 360

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, no major allergens

</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 1350g waxy potatoes
* 100ml vegetable oil
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * ½ tsp ground asafoetida
    * 1 tsp cumin seeds
    * 2 dried hot red chillies
* 2 fresh hot green chillies
* 1 tin chopped tomatoes (400g)
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 1 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp MSG
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Prepare the **spice mixes**.
2. Cut the **potatoes** (skin on) into ~2cm chunks.
3. Slice the **green chillies** crossways into fine slices.

#### B. Cooking

1. Boil the diced **potatoes** for about 25 minutes, until tender. Drain.
2. Heat the **oil** in a large non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat.
3. When hot, add **spice mix 1** and allow to sizzle for a few seconds, until the dried chillies darken.
4. Add the **green chillies** and stir for a moment.
5. Add the boiled potatoes. Stir and fry for a minute or two, until they brown a little.
6. Add the **tomatoes** and **spice mix 2**. Bring to the boil, mashing up the tomatoes as you do so.
7. Cover and simmer gently for 10 minutes.
8. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
