<div class="title-block">
# Sweet lassi
## Unflavoured sweet yoghurt drink
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** https://www.teaforturmeric.com/lassi/

**Rating:** ★★★★☆

**Complexity:** ●○○○○

**Servings:** 6

**Calories/serving:** 430

**Dietary requirements:** Vegetarian, contains dairy

**Other notes:** Simple and tasty. Getting the right consistency can be tricky, still need to experiment.
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 720ml / 24oz whole greek yoghurt
* 360ml / 12oz whole milk
* 240ml / 8oz heavy cream
* 240ml / 8oz sugar
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
2. Blend on high speed until smooth.
3. Check flavour and add sugar as needed. If more volume is needed, add yoghurt and milk in equal measure.
4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours to cool.
