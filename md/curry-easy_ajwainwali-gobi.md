<div class="title-block">
# Ajwainwali gobi
## Cauliflower with ajowan & ginger
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 78

**Rating:** ★★★★☆

**Complexity:** ●○○○○

**Servings:** 6

**Calories/serving:** 216

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums

**Other notes:** Quick, simple, tasty.
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* **Ginger-garlic mix:**
    * 8 tsp ginger
    * 8 cloves garlic
* **Spice mix:**
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * 1 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp MSG
* 3-4 fresh hot green chillies
* 120ml olive or peanut oil
* 2 tsp ajowan seeds (*ajwain*)
* 1135g (40 oz) cauliflower florets, pre-cut
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Peel and finely chop the **garlic** and **ginger**, then combine to form the **ginger-garlic mix**.
2. Prepare the **spice mix**.
3. Slice the **green chillies** crossways into very thin slices.

#### B. Cooking

1. Put the **oil** into a large frying pan over medium heat. When hot, add the **ajowan seeds** and stir a few times.
2. Add the **ginger-garlic mix** and stir for a minute, then add the **spice mix** and stir once.
3. Quickly add the **cauliflower** and **green chillies**, then stir a few times and cover.
4. Cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, for 6-10 minutes, or until the cauliflower is tender enough to break with your fingers.
