# Kodava Mushroom Curry
## Mushroom curry with coconut
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 84

**Rating:** ★★★★★

**Servings:** 4

**Dietary requirements:** Vegetarian, gluten-free, no major allergens

**Other notes:** Very tasty
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 1 × 400ml can of coconut milk
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 1 tsp MSG
    * 2 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp chilli powder
* 675g small button mushrooms
* 3 shallots
* 1-2 fresh green chillies
* 1 lime
* 60 ml olive or groundnut oil
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Leave the **coconut milk** can undisturbed for 24 hours to allow the cream to rise to the top.
2. Carefully open the **coconut milk** can and spoon out the thick cream into a separate bowl.
4. Prepare the **spice mixes**.
3. Cut the **mushrooms** into quarters, then place in a large bowl with **spice mix 1**. Thoroughly rub the spices into the mushrooms, then set aside for 10 minutes.
4. Peel and finely chop the **shallots**.
5. Finely chop the **green chillies**.
6. Juice the **lime**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Put the **oil** in a medium frying pan and set over a medium-high heat. When hot, add the **shallots** and stir-fry until they just start to brown.
2. Take the pan off the heat and add **spice mix 2**. Stir a few times.
3. Return the pan to the heat and add the **green chillies**. Stir a few times.
4. Add the **mushrooms** and cook for 2 minutes.
5. Add the thin **coconut milk**, bring to a simmer, and cook uncovered for 10 minutes.
6. Stir in the thick **coconut cream**, then reduce the heat to very low and simmer uncovered for 2 minutes.
7. Take the pan off the heat and stir in 50 ml of **lime juice**.
8. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
