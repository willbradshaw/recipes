<div class="title-block">
# Matar ki ghugni
## Peas and potatoes, Bihari style
</div>

<div class="preamble">

**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 97

**Rating:** ★★☆☆☆

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 6

**Calories/serving:** 256

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums

</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 800g waxy potatoes
* 60ml vegetable oil
* 1 tsp cumin seeds
* 2 medium onions
* 6 tsp fresh ginger
* 2-6 fresh hot green chillies
* ½ tsp ground turmeric
* 284g (10oz) frozen peas
* **Spice mix:**
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp MSG
    * 1 tsp black pepper
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Prepare the **spice mix**.
2. Cut the **potatoes** (skin on) into ~2cm chunks.
3. Peel and chop the **onions**.
4. Peel and grate the **ginger**.
5. Finely chop the **green chillies**.
6. Defrost the **peas** under running water.

#### B. Cooking

1. Boil the diced **potatoes** for 25-30 minutes, until tender. Drain.
2. Heat the **oil** in a medium non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
3. When hot, add the **cumin seeds** and let sizzle for about 10 seconds.
4. Add the **onions**. Stir and fry for 5-6 minutes, until soft.
5. Add the **ginger**, **green chillies** and **turmeric**. Stir for a minute.
6. Add the **peas**, boiled potatoes and **spice mix**. Stir for 2-3 minutes, sprinkling with a little water if the peas get too dry.
7. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
