<div class="title-block">
# Baghara baingan
## Aubergine in peanut-sesame sauce
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 60

**Rating:** ★★★★★

**Complexity:** ●●●●○

**Servings:** 5

**Calories/serving:** 500

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 3 medium aubergines
* **Marinade mix:**
    * 45 ml vegetable oil
    * 2 tsp salt
    * ½ tsp red chilli powder
* 150g red peanuts
* **Peanut sauce:**
    * 45 ml peanut butter
    * 15 ml tahini
    * 30 ml thick tomato paste
    * 30 ml brown sugar
    * 7½ ml tamarind conc.
* 60 ml vegetable oil
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp mustard seeds
    * 1 tsp cumin seeds
* 6-8 fresh curry leaves
* 1 medium onion
* 3 tsp fresh ginger
* 3 tsp garlic
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * 1 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp ground cumin
    * 1 tsp red chilli powder
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp MSG
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Preheat an oven to 200ºC/390ºF.
2. Chop the **aubergines** into 1x3x3cm chunks, then toss in a large bowl with the **marinade mix**.
3. Finely chop the **peanuts** with a food processor.
4. Mix the chopped peanuts with the rest of the **peanut sauce** in another large bowl with 60 ml water.
5. Peel and finely chop the **onion**, **ginger** and **garlic**.
6. Prepare the **spice mixes**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Spread the aubergine chunks on baking trays and bake for 20 min, stirring at 10 min.
2. Add the **oil** to a large non-stick pan over a medium-high heat. When hot, add **spice mix 1** and cook for a few seconds.
3. Crush the **curry leaves** lightly and add to the pan, then add the **onion** and fry for ~5 min, until browned.
4. Add the **ginger** and **garlic**, cook 2 min, then stir in **spice mix 2** and cook on low for 8-10 min.
5. Stir in the **peanut sauce** and cook on low for 5 min, stirring regularly.
6. Gently stir in the baked aubergine pieces and cook on low for 10 more min.
7. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
