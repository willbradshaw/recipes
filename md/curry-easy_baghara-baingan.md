<div class="title-block">
# Baghara baingan
## Aubergine in peanut-sesame sauce
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 60

**Rating:** ★★★★★

**Servings:** 5

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 3 medium aubergines
* **Marinade mix:**
    * 45 ml vegetable oil
    * 2 tsp salt
    * ½ tsp red chilli powder
* 150g red peanuts
* **Peanut sauce:**
    * 45 ml peanut butter
    * 15 ml tahini
    * 30 ml thick tomato paste
    * 30 ml brown sugar
    * 7½ ml tamarind conc.
* 60 ml vegetable oil
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp mustard seeds
    * 1 tsp cumin seeds
* 6-8 fresh curry leaves
* 1 medium onion
* 3 tsp fresh ginger
* 3 tsp garlic
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * 1 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp ground cumin
    * 1 tsp red chilli powder
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp MSG
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Preheat an oven to 200ºC/390ºF.
2. Chop the **aubergines** into 1x3x3cm chunks, then toss in a large bowl with the **marinade mix**.
3. Finely chop the **peanuts** with a food processor.
4. Mix the chopped peanuts with the rest of the **peanut sauce** in another large bowl with 60 ml water.
5. Peel and finely chop the **onion**, **ginger** and **garlic**.
6. Prepare the **spice mixes**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Spread the aubergine chunks on one or more baking trays and bake in the oven for 20 minutes, stirring after 10 minutes.
2. Add the **oil** to a large non-stick pan over a medium-high heat. When hot, add **spice mix 1** and cook for a few seconds.
3. Crush the **curry leaves** lightly and add to the pan, then add the **onion**. Stir and fry for about 5 minutes, until the onion browns.
4. Add the **ginger** and **garlic** and cook for 2 minutes, then stir in **spice mix 2** and cook on a low heat for 8-10 minutes.
5. Stir in the **peanut sauce** and cook on a low heat for 5 minutes, stirring regularly.
6. Gently stir in the baked aubergine pieces and cook for 10 more minutes on a low heat.
7. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
