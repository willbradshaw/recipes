<div class="title-block">
# Ferar aloo
## Ginger-chilli peanuts and potatoes
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Prashad*, p. 115

**Rating:** ★★★★★

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 4

**Calories/serving:** 600

**Dietary requirements:** Vegetarian, gluten-free, contains peanuts

**Other notes:** Quick, easy, very tasty
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 700g red-skinned (or other waxy) potatoes
* 5cm root ginger
* 3-4 fresh green chillies
* 160g raw red-skinned peanuts
* **Spice mix:**
    * 3 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp sugar
    * 2 tsp MSG
* 100g unsalted butter
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Cut the **potatoes** (skin on) into ~2cm chunks.
2. Crush the **ginger** using a pestle and mortar to make a fine pulp.
3. Slice the **chillies** into fine rings.
4. Coarsely chop the **peanuts**.
5. Prepare the **spice mix**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Boil the diced **potatoes** for 25-30 minutes, until tender. Drain.
2. Melt the **butter** in a large pan over a medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and stir in the **ginger pulp** and **chilli rings**.
3. Cook for 2 minutes, then stir in the **peanuts**.
4. Increase the heat to low/medium, then stir in the **spice mix**
5. Stir and fry for 2 minutes, then gently stir in the **potatoes** until they are coated in the spiced butter.
6. Cover the pan and leave to cook over a low heat for 2 minutes.
7. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
