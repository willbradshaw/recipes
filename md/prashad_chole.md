# Chole
## Cinnamon-spice chickpea curry
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Prashad*, p. 142

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Servings:** 4

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums

**Other notes:** Fairly heavily modified from cookbook, mainly for convenience
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 3 × 16oz (~450g) tins of chickpeas
* 100ml sunflower oil
* 1 medium onion
* **Spice mix:**
    * 2 tsp ground coriander
    * 2 tsp red chilli powder
    * 2 tsp ground cinnamon
    * 1 tsp ground cloves
    * 1 tsp ground black pepper
    * 1 tsp ground cumin
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp turmeric
    * 1 tsp sugar
* 1 tsp cumin seeds
* 6 dried bay leaves
* 4cm root ginger
* 1 tin peeled or chopped tomatoes
* 2 handfuls fresh coriander/cilantro
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Rinse and drain the **chickpeas**.
2. Finely chop the **tomatoes**, **onion** and **fresh coriander**.
3. Prepare the **spice mix**.
4. Crush or finely chop the **ginger**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Place the **chickpeas** in a large pan with 500ml of warm water and boil for 5 minutes over a high heat, then remove from the heat and set aside.
2. Heat the **oil** in a large pan over a medium heat, then add the **cumin seeds** and fry for 1 minute, until they start to brown.
3. Increase the heat to high, add the **onion** and fry until dark brown. To do this, let cook covered for 2 minutes, then stir and recover; repeat until dark brown.
4. Stir in the **tomanoes**, **ginger**, **spice mix**, **chickpeas** and **fresh coriander**, then cover and cook for 5-10 minutes, until the chickpeas are tender.
5. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
