<div class="title-block">
# Saadi khumbi
## Simple mushrooms
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 87

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 4

**Calories/serving:** 220

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, no major allergens
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 900g mixed button mushrooms
* **Spice mix:**
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp MSG
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * 1 tsp red chilli powder
* 2 medium onions
* 2-4 fresh hot green chillies
* 60 ml olive or peanut oil
* 1 tsp whole brown mustard seeds
* 3 tsp ground coriander
* 50 ml water
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Halve the **mushrooms**, quartering any larger ones.
2. Prepare the **spice mix**.
3. Place the **mushrooms** in a large bowl, sprinkle over the **spice mix**, and thoroughly rub the spices in. Set aside for about 10 minutes.
4. Peel and roughly chop the **onions**.
5. Finely chop the **green chillies**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Put the **oil** in a medium frying pan and set over medium-high heat.
2. When hot, add the **mustard seeds**. As soon as they start to pop, add the **green chillies** and **onions**. Stir and fry until the onions just start to brown, 3-4 minutes.
3. Add the **ground coriander** and stir once.
4. Add the **mushrooms** and their accumulated liquid. Stir over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes.
5. Add the **water** and bring to a simmer. Cover and simmer on low heat for 10 minutes.
6. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
