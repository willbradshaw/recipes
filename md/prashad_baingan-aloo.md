<div class="title-block">
# Baingan aloo
## Aubergine and potatoes
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Prashad*, p. 111

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 4

**Calories/serving:** 375

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, no major allergens

**Other notes:** Simple and straightforward.
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* **Masala paste:**
    * 2-3 fresh green chillies
    * 3cm root ginger
    * pinch of salt
* 2 medium aubergines
* 450g medium red-skinned (or other waxy) potatoes
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp cumin seeds
    * 1 tsp brown mustard seeds
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 2 tsp turmeric
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp medium red chilli powder
    * 2 tsp sugar
    * 9 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp ground cumin
* 100ml sunflower oil
* ½ tsp asafetida
* 3 medium tomatoes
* 2 handfuls of fresh coriander
* 150ml boiling water
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Crush the **masala paste** ingredients together with a pestle and mortar (or a blender) to make a fine paste.
2. Cut the **aubergines** into 2cm cubes.
3. Peel and cut the **potatoes** into 2cm cubes.
4. Prepare the **spice mixes**.
5. Roughly chop the **tomatoes** and **fresh coriander**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a large pan for a minute over a medium heat, then add **spice mix 1**.
2. When the mustard seeds start to pop, remove from the heat. Immediately add the **asafetida**, **aubergines** and **potatoes**, stir, then return to the heat.
3. Stir in the **masala paste** and **spice mix 2**, then cook covered for 3-4 minutes over a low heat.
4. Increase the heat to bring to a simmering boil, then simmer uncovered over a low heat again for 5-6 minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. Stir in the **tomatoes** and **fresh coriander**, then cover the pan and simmer for a further 5 minutes, or until the potatoes are cooked through.
6. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
