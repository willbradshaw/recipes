<div class="title-block">
# Sukhu bhindi aloo
## Okra and potatoes with mustard seeds
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Prashad*, p. 124

**Rating:** ★★★★☆

**Complexity:** ●●●○○

**Servings:** 4

**Calories/serving:** 410

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums

**Other notes:** Wash okra and check for quality before drying.
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 650g okra
* **Masala paste:**
    * 4-6 fresh green chillies
    * 4-6 cloves of garlic
    * pinch of salt
* 450g waxy potatoes
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 2 tsp fenugreek seeds
    * 1 tsp brown mustard seeds
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp turmeric
* **Spice mix 3:**
    * 3 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp ground cumin
    * 1 tsp red chilli powder
* 1 handful fresh coriander
* 125ml sunflower oil
* ½ tsp asafetida
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Wash and dry the **okra**, then trim, cut in half lengthways, and chop into ~2cm chunks. Leave out uncovered for ~24 hours to dry out.
2. Crush the **masala paste** together with a pestle and mortar.
3. Peel the **potatoes** and cut into chunks roughly 1 x 4 cm.
4. Prepare the **spice mixes**.
5. Finely chop the **fresh coriander**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a large frying pan for 1 minute over a medium heat.
2. Add **spice mix 1**, swirl in the oil, then quickly add the **asafetida** and **potatoes**.
3. Increase the heat to high and fry for 1 minute, then stir in the **okra** and return the heat to medium.
4. Gently stir in **spice mix 2**, then cover the pan and leave to cook for 3 minutes.
5. Gently stir in the **masala paste**, **spice mix 3** and the **fresh coriander**, being careful not to break the delicate okra.
6. Cover and cook until the potatoes are cooked through (about 5 minutes).
7. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
