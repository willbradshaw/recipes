<div class="title-block">
# Khumbi ki tarkari
## Wild mushrooms in coconut sauce
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 36

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Complexity:** ●●●○○

**Servings:** 6

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 1kg mixed mushrooms (e.g. hon shimeji, shiitake, oyster)
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp whole brown mustard seeds
    * 1 tsp whole cumin seeds
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 1 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp red chilli powder
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp MSG
* 6 medium shallots
* 6 cloves garlic
* 2-4 fresh hot green chillies
* 90ml olive or peanut oil
* 14-16 fresh curry leaves
* salt
* 320ml (11 fl oz) coconut milk, from a well-shaken can
* 4 tsp lime juice
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Wipe the **mushrooms** clean with damp kitchen paper, then chop and trim into smaller pieces, roughly 2.5 cm on a side.
2. Prepare the **spice mixes**.
3. Peel and finely chop the **shallots** and **garlic**.
4. Finely chop the **green chillies**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Put the **oil** in a large, preferably non-stick pan and set over a medium-high heat. When hot, add **spice mix 1**.
2. As soon as the mustard seeds begin to pop, crush and add the **curry leaves**, followed immediately by the **shallots**, **green chillies** and **garlic**.
3. Stir for about 3 minutes, then add all the **mushrooms**. Stir gently for 4-6 minutes, until the mushrooms appear wet.
4. Add **spice mix 2**, then stir and cook for another 4 minutes.
5. Pour in the **coconut milk**, bring to a simmer, then simmer gently for 4 minutes.
6. Stir in the **lime juice** and taste to check the balance of seasonings.
