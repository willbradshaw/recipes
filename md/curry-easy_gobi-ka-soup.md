<div class="title-block">
# Gobi ka soup
## Cauliflower soup
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 19

**Rating:** ★★☆☆☆

**Complexity:** ●●●○○

**Servings:** 4

**Calories/serving:** 245

**Dietary requirements:** Vegetarian, gluten-free, contains alliums & dairy

**Other notes:** Solidly meh
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 3 tablespoons olive or peanut oil
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp whole cumin seeds
    * 1 tsp whole fennel seeds
* 1 onion (about 195g)
* 1 potato (about 195g)
* **Masala paste:**
    * 3 tsp fresh ginger
    * 3 cloves garlic
    * 1 fresh hot green chilli
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 2 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp ground cumin
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * 2 tsp red chilli powder
* 285g cauliflower florets
* 2 medium tomatoes
* **Spice mix 3:**
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp MSG
* 1 generous handful fresh coriander
* Freshly ground black pepper
* 2 teaspoons lime juice
* 60ml double cream
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Peel and chop the **onion**, **potato**, and **tomatoes**.
2. Crush the **masala paste** ingredients together into a paste.
3. Prepare the **spice mixes**.
4. Finely chop the **fresh coriander**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a large pan over a medium-high heat.
2. When hot, add **spice mix 1** and cook for a few seconds.
3. Add the **onion** and **potato**, then stir and sauté for 5 minutes.
4. Add the **masala paste** and stir for 1 minute.
5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and add **spice mix 2**. Stir for 1 minute.
6. Add the **cauliflower**, **tomatoes**, **fresh coriander**, and **spice mix 3**. Stir for 1 minute.
7. Add 1L of hot water, stir and bring to the boil over a medium-high heat, then cover and simmer gently for 25 minutes.
9. Remove from the heat and allow to cool slightly, then blend and strain to produce a smooth texture.
10. Season to taste with **black pepper** and **lime juice**, then stir in the **cream**.
11. Reheat gently to serve.
