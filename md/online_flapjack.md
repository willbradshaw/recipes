# Flapjack
## Traditional British oat cake
</div>

<div class="preamble">

**Rating:** ★★★★☆

**Complexity:** ●○○○○

**Servings:** 6

**Calories/serving:** 930

**Dietary requirements:** Vegetarian, contains dairy

**Other notes:** Simple and tasty, heavily adapted.
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 200g fine rolled porridge oats
* 200g old fashioned porridge oats
* 100g plain flour
* 200g sultanas (or raisins if sultanas are unavailable)
* 200g unsalted butter
* 200g golden syrup
* 200g light brown sugar
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 170°C/340°F.
2. Grease a 9-inch square cake tin and line with baking parchment.
3. Mix the **fine oats**, **old-fashioned oats**, **flour** and **sultanas** in a large bowl.
4. Mix the **butter**, **syrup** and **sugar** in a small saucepan and cook over a low heat until melted and smooth.
5. Pour the syrup mixture into the bowl and stir until thoroughly combined, with no dry patches at all.
6. Spread the mixture in the cake tin, pressing down until it is level and compact.
7. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until set and golden.
8. Leave to cool for 10 minutes, then cut into slices with a sharp knife while still in the tin.
9. Leave to cool completely, then remove from the tin and re-slice.
10. Store in an airtight container for up to 10 days.
