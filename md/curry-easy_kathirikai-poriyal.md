<div class="title-block">
# Kathirikai poriyal
## Stir-fried aubergines, Tamil Nadu style
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 58

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Complexity:** ●○○○○

**Servings:** 6

**Calories/serving:** 275

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 4 medium aubergines (about 1400g)
* **Spice mix:**
    * 6 tsp ground coriander
    * 3 tsp red chilli powder
    * 1 tsp ground cumin
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp MSG
* 100ml olive or peanut oil
* 1 tsp whole brown mustard seeds
* 16-20 fresh curry leaves
* 2 onions (about 200g)
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Cut the tops off the **aubergines** and cut into 2.5 × 2 cm (1 × ¾ inch) dice. Soak the pieces in a bowl of water until ready to cook.
2. Prepare the **spice mix**.
3. Peel and chop the **onions**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Pour the **oil** into a large, preferably non-stick frying pan set over a medium-high heat. When hot, add the **mustard seeds**.
2. As soon as they start to pop, crush the **curry leaves** lightly in your hand and add them. Quickly follow with the **onions**. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, until lightly browned at the edges.
3. Drain and pat dry the **aubergines**, then add to the pan. Stir for a minute.
4. Add the **spice mix**. Stir to combine, then cover. Cook for about 6 minutes, then check and stir. Cover and repeat a few times, for 20-25 minutes total.
5. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
