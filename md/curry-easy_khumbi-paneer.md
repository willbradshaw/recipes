<div class="title-block">
# Khumbi paneer
## Fresh paneer with peas and mushrooms
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 261

**Rating:** ★★★★☆

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 6

**Calories/serving:** 488

**Dietary requirements:** Vegetarian, gluten-free, contains dairy
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* **Sauce mixture:**
    * 56g (2 oz) raw cashews
    * 1 × 400g (14 oz) tin crushed tomatoes
    * 2 tsp ground cumin
    * 2 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * ½ tsp ground cloves
    * ½ tsp ground cinnamon
    * ½ tsp ground cardamom
    * 2 tsp MSG
* 225g (8 oz) mushrooms
* 284g (10 oz) frozen peas
* 3 tsp fresh ginger
* 510g (18 oz) paneer
* 2 tsp salt
* 60ml olive or vegetable oil
* **Spice mix:**
    * ½ tsp ground asafoetida
    * 1 tsp whole cumin seeds
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Defrost the **peas** in warm water.
2. Wipe down and quarter the **mushrooms**.
3. Peel and crush the **ginger**.
4. Cut the **paneer** into 2cm (¾ inch) cubes and toss in a bowl with the **salt**.
5. Combine all the **spice mixture** ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
6. Prepare the **spice mix**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a medium, non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
2. When hot, add the **spice mix**. Let the seeds sizzle for 10 seconds.
3. Add the **mushrooms** and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, until lightly browned and glossy.
4. Add the **peas** and **ginger** and stir for 1 minute.
5. Add the **paneer** cubes and **salt** and stir for 2 minutes.
6. Add the **sauce mixture**. Stir and bring to a simmer, then heat gently for about 3 minutes, until everything is warmed through and well mixed.
7. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
