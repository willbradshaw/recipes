<div class="title-block">
# Wattana and flower
## Pea and cauliflower curry
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Prashad*, p. 118

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Servings:** 4

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, no major allergens

**Other notes:** n/a
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* **Masala paste:**
    * 3-5 fresh green chillies
    * 5cm root ginger
    * pinch of salt
* 250g frozen petits pois peas
* 1 medium cauliflower
* 2 medium fresh tomatoes
* 1 handful fresh coriander/cilantro
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp cumin seeds
    * 1 tsp brown mustard seeds
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp turmeric
    * 3 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp sugar
* 100ml sunflower oil
* ½ tsp asafetida
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Crush the **masala paste** together with a pestle and mortar.
2. Thaw the **peas** in warm water.
3. Cut the **cauliflower** into small florets.
4. Finely chop the **tomatoes** and **fresh coriander**.
5. Prepare the **spice mixes**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a large frying pan for 1 minute over a medium heat.
2. Add **spice mix 1**, swirl in the oil, then reduce the heat to low and add the **asafetida**.
3. Add the **cauliflower**, then increase the heat to medium and add the **masala paste** and **spice mix 2**.
4. Cover and cook for 10 minutes, stirring every few minutes.
5. Stir in the **peas**, **tomatoes** and **fresh coriander**, then re-cover and cook for 5 more minutes.
6. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
