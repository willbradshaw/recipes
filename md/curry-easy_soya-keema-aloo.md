<div class="title-block">
# Soya keema aloo
## Soya chunks with potatoes
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 152

**Rating:** ★★★★★

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 8

**Calories/serving:** 325

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 230g soya chunks
* 680g waxy potatoes
* 2 medium onions
* **Masala paste:**
    * 5 tsp ginger
    * 6 cloves garlic
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp ground black pepper
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 1 tsp salt
    * 1 tsp MSG
* **Spice mix 3:**
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * 2 tsp red chilli powder
    * 3 tsp ground coriander
* 1 handful fresh coriander
* 125ml vegetable oil
* ½ tsp ground asafoetida
* 1 × 400g tin of peeled or chopped tomatoes
* 1 tsp garam masala
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Soak the **soya chunks** in 500ml water for 5 minutes. Drain, saving the soaking water.
2. Cut the **potatoes** into thin (0.5cm) chunks, without peeling.
3. Peel and finely chop the **onions**.
4. Crush the **masala paste** ingredients into a smooth paste.
5. Prepare the **spice mixes**.
6. Finely chop the **fresh coriander**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a large frying pan over a medium-high heat.
2. Add the **potatoes** and fry until golden brown on both sides — they don't need to cook through.
3. Remove the potatoes with a slotted spoon, drain on kitchen paper, then toss lightly with **spice mix 1** in a large bowl
4. Re-heat the pan, then add the **asafoetida**, followed by the **onions**. Stir and fry for 8 minutes, until the onion browns at the edges.
5. Add the **tomatoes** and **spice mix 2**, then stir and fry for 6 minutes or until the tomatoes are dark and soft.
6. Add the **masala paste** and stir for 3 minutes.
7. Reduce the heat to medium-low and stir in **spice mix 3**.
8. Add the soya granules, their soaking liquid, and the potatoes. Bring to a simmer, then cover and cook on a low heat for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
9. Stir in the **garam masala** and **fresh coriander**.
