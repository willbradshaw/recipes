<div class="title-block">
# Matar gobi
## Cauliflower with peas
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Curry Easy Vegetarian*, p. 77

**Rating:** ★★★★☆

**Complexity:** ●●○○○

**Servings:** 6

**Calories/serving:** 218

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, no major allergens
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 680g (24 oz) pre-chopped cauliflower florets
* 284g (10 oz) frozen peas
* 2 tsp peeled fresh ginger
* 2-4 fresh green chillies
* Generous handful fresh coriander
* **Spice mix 1:**
    * 1 tsp ground asafoetida
    * 2 tsp whole brown mustard seeds
* **Spice mix 2:**
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp MSG
    * 1 tsp ground turmeric
    * 2 tsp red chilli powder
    * 4 tsp ground coriander
* 100ml olive or peanut oil
* 1 × 411g (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Put the **cauliflower** florets in a large bowl filled with water. Set aside for 10-30 minutes, then drain.
2. Defrost the **peas** in warm water.
3. Peel and finely chop the **ginger**.
4. Slice the **green chillies** crossways into thin rounds.
5. Finely chop the **fresh coriander**.
6. Prepare the **spice mixes**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a large, preferably non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat.
2. When hot, add **spice mix 1**. As soon as the seeds start to pop, add the **green chillies** and stir once.
3. Add the **tomatoes**, **spice mix 2**, **ginger** and **fresh coriander**. Stir and fry for a few minutes until the tomato thickens into a paste.
4. Add the **cauliflower** and **peas**. Stir a few times, bring to a simmer, then lower the heat to medium-low.
5. Cover and cook gently, stirring now and then, for about 10 minutes, or until the cauliflower is tender.
6. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to infuse.
