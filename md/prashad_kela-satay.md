<div class="title-block">
# Kela satay
## Banana satay
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Prashad*, p. 138

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Servings:** 4

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums

**Other notes:** Not bad, but not worth the effort.
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* 3-6 cloves of garlic
* 4cm root ginger
* **Spice mix:**
    * 3 teaspoons ground coriander
    * 3 teaspoons red chilli powder
    * 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    * 1 teaspoon turmeric
    * 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    * 2 teaspoons salt
    * 1 teaspoon sugar
* 100g red-skinned unsalted peanuts
* 2 teaspoons chickpea flour
* 1 handful fresh coriander
* 100ml sunflower oil
* 4 green unripe bananas
* 200ml warm water
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Crush or finely chop the **ginger** and **garlic**.
2. Finely chop or blend the **peanuts** into a coarse powder.
3. Prepare the **spice mix**.
4. Finely chop the **fresh coriander**.
5. Combine the **peanuts**, **chickpea flour**, **ginger**, **garlic**, **spice mix**, **fresh coriander** and **oil** in a large bowl, then mix with your hands into an even paste.
6. Rinse and dry the **bananas** (do not peel), then chop into thirds.
7. Cut each **banana** piece lengthways into quarters, leaving the last centimetre intact to hold them together.
8. Gently open the **banana** pieces and spread the cut surfaces with the spicy peanut paste. 

#### B. Cooking

1. Place the **banana** pieces in a large lidded pan, then scoop any leftover paste into the pan between the banana pieces.
2. Place the pan over a high heat and cook for 1 minute.
3. Pour the **water** into the paste bowl and swirl, then pour into the pan between the bananas.
4. Reduce the heat to medium, cover and cook for 1 minute.
5. Reduce the heat to very low and leave to cook slowly for 13-15 minutes, carefully turning the banana pieces every 3-5 minutes to ensure they are completely infused with the spicy sauce.
6. Serve immediately while hot.
