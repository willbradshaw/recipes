<div class="title-block">
# Soya matar keema
## Soya mince with peas
</div>

<div class="preamble">
**Source:** *Prashad At Home*, p. 63

**Rating:** ★★★☆☆

**Servings:** 4

**Calories/serving:** 335

**Dietary requirements:** Vegan, gluten-free, contains alliums

**Other notes:** Okay as a high-protein change from paneer. Don't overdo the soya mince.
</div>

<div class="ingredients-container">

### Ingredients

::: {.ingredients}
* **Masala paste:**
    * 6-8 cloves of garlic
    * 5cm root ginger
    * pinch of salt
* 75ml sunflower oil
* 3cm cinnamon stick
* 3 large Indian bay leaves
* 1 tsp mustard seeds
* ½ tsp asafetida
* 2 medium onions, diced
* 3 medium vine tomatoes, diced, or 1 tin chopped or peeled
* **Spice mix:**
    * 1 tsp red chilli powder
    * 2 tsp garam masala
    * 1 tsp turmeric
    * 2 tsp salt
    * 2 tsp ground coriander
    * 1 tsp ground cumin
* 120g frozen petits pois peas
* 125ml hot water
* 100-120g dried soya mince
* 1 handful fresh coriander
:::

</div>

<div class="instructions">

### Instructions

#### A. Preparation

1. Soak the **soya mince** in 1 L of warm water for 20 minutes.
2. Thaw the **peas** in warm water.
3. Crush the **masala paste** together with a pestle and mortar.
4. Prepare the **spice mix**.
5. Chop the **onions**, **tomatoes** and **fresh coriander**.

#### B. Cooking

1. Heat the **oil** in a large pan for 2 minutes over a medium heat.
2. Add the **cinnamon stick** and **bay leaves** and stir through the warm oil for about 30 seconds.
3. Add the **mustard seeds** and swirl once more.
4. Once the mustard seeds start to pop, add the **asafetida** and **onions** and stir through.
5. Increase the heat to high and fry the onions for 4 minutes, stirring regularly.
6. Add the **tomatoes**, cover and cook for 5 minutes, checking and stirring occasionally.
7. Add the **masala paste** and **spice mix** & stir through.
8. Add the **peas** and **hot water** and stir through.
9. Lower the heat to medium and cook for 4 minutes.
10. Drain and add the **soya mince** along with the **fresh coriander**. Stir through and cook briefly.
11. Remove from the heat and leave covered for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse.
